### Ramadan Dessert Menu 2026

#### Hazelnut Dream Cake
- **Description:** Hazelnut Dream Cake in a bowl - crunchy choux and craqueline layered with hazelnut praline, caramel, hazelnut diplomat cream, and hazelnut sauce. Finished with caramelized hazelnuts on top. Rich, nutty, and super comforting.
- **Components:** Craqueline, Hazelnut sauce, Caramelized hazelnut, Hazelnut diplomat, Choux, Hazelnut praline, Hazelnut skin.
- **Caloric Count:** 550 - 650 kcal
- **Allergens:** Nuts, Dairy, Eggs, Gluten, Soy

#### Originale Banana Pudding
- **Description:** Creamy banana custard layered with vanilla wafers and fresh bananas. Light, comforting, and not too sweet.
- **Key Ingredients:** Fresh banana, Eggnog, Banana cream.
- **Caloric Count:** 300 - 380 kcal
- **Allergens:** Dairy, Eggs, Gluten, Soy

#### Mango Passion Baba
- **Description:** Soft baba sponge soaked in fragrant passion fruit syrup, layered with rich creme diplomat, mango-passionfruit compote, and finished with fresh tropical notes. Light, juicy, and creamy in every bite.
- **Note:** Baba is a classic French sponge cake soaked in syrup, later popularized in Italy (pronounced bah-bah, not papa).
- **Components:** Baba syrup, Baba, Diplomat cream, Mango compote, Lime zest.
- **Caloric Count:** 380 - 430 kcal
- **Allergens:** Gluten, Eggs, Dairy
